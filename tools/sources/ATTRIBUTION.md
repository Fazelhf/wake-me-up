# Transit data sources

The bundled Tehran transit datasets under `app/src/main/assets/transit/`
are generated by `tools/build_transit_assets.py` from these vendored
snapshots:

- `tehran_metro_stations.json` — from
  [mostafa-kheibary/tehran-metro-data](https://github.com/mostafa-kheibary/tehran-metro-data)
  (ISC license). 150 Tehran Metro stations with Persian/English names,
  coordinates, line membership and station adjacency.
- `tehran_brt_stations.json` — from
  [MohammadAmin-Andy/iran-public-transport](https://github.com/MohammadAmin-Andy/iran-public-transport)
  (`tehran/bus/brt`, Open Database License v1.0). Tehran BRT lines 1 and 4
  station lists, Persian names and adjacency. Most BRT entries carry no
  coordinates in the source; the converter fills them from source anchors,
  co-located metro stations and street-corridor interpolation.

Snapshots taken 2026-07-17.
